The Crystal Collector Game!


A random number will be given to you at the beginming of the game.


There are 4 crystals below. By clicking one of them, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


Win the game by matching your total score with the random number. If you go over the random number, you will lose the game.


The value of the crystal is a mystery number until you click it.


* Each time the game restarts, the values of the crystals will change.
